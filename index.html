about me

Hello!
My name is Stephanie. I am a visual artist and junior web developer who is always looking to expand her skill set! From a very young age, I have felt driven to create whatever I can, from drawing on my notes in school, to studying painting and design, and now to coding, which is quickly becoming a creative outlet for me. My paintings are quite surreal, and I'm very interested to see how that will manifest in my web design as my work develops.
As I continue to study code, I will be making both functional and design changes to my portfolio. In the meantime, here is where I'm starting out! Feel free to click around and make sure all of my links are working properly.
And of course, thank you for taking the time to visit my portfolio!
